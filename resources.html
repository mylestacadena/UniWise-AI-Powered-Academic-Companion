üè´ School Background

Senior High School within Bacoor Elementary School was established in 2016. It is one of the five public senior high schools in the Division of Bacoor City. It started catering to students under the STEM strand and later opened the HUMSS strand in 2020.

üåç Community Profile

SHS within Bacoor ES was first housed in SHS Dulong Bayan together with two other SHSs. In June 2021, the school transferred and was housed within Bacoor ES. It was first named SHS Alima since Bacoor ES is situated in Barangay Alima, then later renamed to its current title.
